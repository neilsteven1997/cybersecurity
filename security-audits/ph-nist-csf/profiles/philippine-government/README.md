# Philippine Government Profile

**NIST CSF 2.0 Organizational Profile**  
Hypothetical audit for Philippine government agencies (national and local).

**Scope**  
Focuses on governance, critical infrastructure protection, and compliance with Republic Act 10173 (Data Privacy Act), DICT guidelines, and national cybersecurity strategy.

**Files in this folder**
- `audit-ph-government.md` – Full NIST CSF assessment (current state, target state, gaps)
- `recommendations.md` – Prioritised action plan with owners and timelines
- `evidence/` – Supporting notes, screenshots, and references

**Key Focus Areas**  
GOVERN (GV), IDENTIFY (ID), and SUPPLY CHAIN risk management.

Created as part of hypothetical security audit project – March 2026.
